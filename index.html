Student Welfare Association

Empowering Students. Building Community. Transforming Lives.

Welcome to the Student Welfare Association (SWA) at Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University of Science and Technology (JOOUST).

We are dedicated to supporting students through financial assistance, academic programs, welfare support, and community engagement.

About Us

Our Mission

To create a supportive environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and community engagement for all students of JOOUST.

Our Vision

To be the leading student organization that ensures the welfare and well-being of every student through innovative programs and sustainable support systems.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We conduct our affairs with honesty and ethical standards.
  • Inclusivity: We welcome all students regardless of background.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek new ways to improve our services.
  • Impact: We measure success by positive changes in students' lives.

Learn more about our history, team, and achievements.
