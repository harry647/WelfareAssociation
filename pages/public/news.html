SWA Launches New Scholarship Program

Student Welfare Association is proud to announce a new scholarship program for academically excellent students facing financial challenges. The program will provide full fee payment support for 10 students this semester.

Eligibility criteria include: minimum GPA of 3.0, active membership for at least one semester, and demonstrated financial need. Applications are now open and will close on April 30, 2025.
