Why Volunteer?

Your time and skills can transform lives. Join our volunteer program and be part of something meaningful. Volunteering with SWA is a great way to give back to the community, develop new skills, and make lasting connections with fellow students.

Whether you have a few hours a week or want to take on a bigger role, there's a place for you in our volunteer program.

Volunteer Benefits

Gain Experience

Build valuable skills in leadership, communication, event management, and teamwork that will benefit your future career.

Network

Connect with fellow students, alumni, industry professionals, and community leaders. Build relationships that last.

Certificate

Receive a certificate of volunteer service that recognizes your contribution and can boost your resume.

Help Others

Make a real impact in the lives of students in need and contribute to positive change in your community.

Learn New Skills

Develop practical skills in project planning, fundraising, public speaking, and more through hands-on experience.

Personal Growth

Gain confidence, self-esteem, and a sense of purpose by contributing to something bigger than yourself.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much time do I need to commit?

Time commitments vary by role. Most positions require 2-5 hours per week. You can choose a role that fits your schedule.

Is volunteer work mandatory for members?

No, volunteering is completely voluntary. However, we encourage all members to participate in at least one community service activity per semester.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes! All volunteers who complete their commitment receive a certificate of service that can be added to your resume.

Can I volunteer if I'm not a member?

Yes, we welcome non-members to volunteer. However, we encourage you to also become a member to enjoy all SWA benefits.
