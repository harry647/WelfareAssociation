About Student Welfare Association

Empowering students, building community, transforming lives

Welcome to SWA

The Student Welfare Association (SWA) is a student-led organization dedicated to the holistic welfare and development of all students at Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University of Science and Technology (JOOUST).

Founded on January 10, 2025 by 23 concerned students, SWA exists to address the multifaceted challenges faced by students in campus life. Our mission is to create a supportive environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and personally.

We believe that when students support each other, amazing things happen. Through our comprehensive welfare programs, financial assistance schemes, community service initiatives, and academic support services, we aim to ensure that no student is left behind due to financial, social, or personal challenges.

Our Mission

To create a supportive environment that fosters academic excellence, personal growth, and community engagement for all students of JOOUST. We are dedicated to providing resources, support, and a community where every student can thrive academically and personally.

Our Vision

To be the leading student organization that ensures the welfare and well-being of every student through innovative programs and sustainable support systems. We envision a campus where no student is left behind due to financial, social, or personal challenges.

Our Core Values

These values guide everything we do at SWA:

Integrity

We conduct our affairs with honesty, transparency, and ethical standards. We believe in doing the right thing, even when no one is watching.

Inclusivity

We welcome all students regardless of background, course, year of study, or circumstances. Every student matters and has a place in our community.

Innovation

We continuously seek new ways to improve our services and programs. We embrace creativity and forward thinking to address emerging challenges.

Impact

We measure success by the positive changes we create in students' lives. Every action we take is focused on making a meaningful difference.

Solidarity

We stand together in times of need. Our strength comes from the unity and support we show to one another as a community.

Growth

We are committed to the continuous development of our members, organization, and programs. Personal and collective growth is essential to our mission.

Our History

SWA was born out of a recognition that students face numerous challenges during their university journey. Here's our story:

January 10, 2025

Inception

SWA was founded by 23 concerned students who recognized the urgent need to address challenges faced by fellow students, including financial constraints, housing issues, and lack of academic support.

March 2025

First Executive Committee

The first executive committee was elected, comprising Chairman, Secretary, Treasurer, Organizing Secretary, and Consultant. This team set the foundation for SWA's operations and governance.

June 2025

Launch of Financial Programs

SWA introduced its interest-free loan program and welfare support system to assist members facing financial difficulties.

September 2025

Membership Growth

Membership grew to over 50 students, demonstrating the significant need for such an organization within the JOOUST community.

October 2025

Scholarship Program Launch

SWA launched its scholarship program to support academically excellent students facing financial challenges.

2025-2026

Community Impact

Over Ksh 50,000 disbursed in loans and welfare support. Multiple community service projects completed. Partnerships established with local businesses and organizations.

Our Objectives

SWA is committed to achieving the following objectives:

  • To provide financial support to students in need through our welfare programs and interest-free loans
  • To organize academic support programs, workshops, and mentorship opportunities
  • To promote community service and volunteerism among students
  • To advocate for student rights and welfare within the university administration
  • To foster networking and collaboration among students
  • To collaborate with university administration for improved student welfare
  • To provide mental health support and counseling services
  • To organize social events that build community and foster friendships
  • To develop leadership skills through committee participation and event organization
  • To create sustainable systems that ensure long-term support for students

Our Programs & Services

SWA offers a comprehensive range of programs designed to support students:

Academic Support

Tutoring services, study groups, and academic mentorship programs to help students excel in their studies.

Interest-free loans, emergency funds, and scholarship opportunities for members in need.

Welfare Programs

Mental health support, counseling services, and wellness programs for holistic student development.

Community Service

Volunteer opportunities and outreach programs that allow students to give back to society.

Career Development

Career workshops, resume building, internship placement, and networking events.

Events & Activities

Regular social events, team building activities, and cultural celebrations.
