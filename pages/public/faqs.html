Membership

All registered students of Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University of Science and Technology (JOOUST) are eligible to become members of the Student Welfare Association. This includes undergraduate, postgraduate, full-time, and part-time students from all departments and faculties.

To become a member, visit our Student Portal and complete the registration form. You'll need to provide your student ID and basic information. Registration is completely FREE!

Registration is free. However, members are required to make monthly contributions as agreed upon by the general assembly. The minimum contribution amount is determined each semester (currently Ksh 500 per month).

As an SWA member, you enjoy:

  • Access to interest-free loans
  • Emergency welfare support
  • Academic mentorship programs
  • Career development workshops
  • Networking events
  • Community service opportunities
  • Member-only resources

Contributions

The minimum monthly contribution is Ksh 500 per month. This amount is determined at the beginning of each semester by the executive committee and approved by the general assembly.

We accept payments via:

  • M-Pesa: Use the paybill number provided in the member portal
  • Bank Transfer: Direct deposit to SWA's account (details in member portal)
  • Cash Payment: Pay directly at the SWA office during office hours

Members who miss contributions will receive a reminder. Extended non-payment (more than 2 months) may result in temporary suspension of benefits until contributions are up to date. However, we always encourage members to communicate with us if facing difficulties.

Yes! Members are welcome to contribute more than the minimum amount. Higher contributions help support more students in need and may increase your loan eligibility.

Loans

Log in to the Members Portal and navigate to the Loan section. Fill out the application form with the required information and submit. Your application will be reviewed by the finance committee within 3-5 business days.

To qualify for a loan, you must:

  • Be an active member for at least 3 months
  • Have at least 3 months of continuous contribution history
  • Have no outstanding loans
  • Have a good standing (no disciplinary issues)
  • Provide a guarantor who is also an active member

Our loans have a competitive interest rate of 5% per month on reducing balance. This is significantly lower than commercial lenders and banks.

Loans are limited to a maximum of 3 times your total contributions. First-time borrowers can access up to Ksh 5,000.

Loans must be repaid within 6 months through monthly installments. Early repayment is allowed without penalty.
