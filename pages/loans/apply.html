Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a loan?

Loan applications are typically processed within 24-48 hours. Once approved, funds are disbursed directly to your M-Pesa or bank account.

What happens if I can't repay on time?

If you're unable to repay on time, please contact the finance team immediately. Late payments may incur additional interest and affect your future borrowing eligibility.

Can I apply for a second loan while repaying the first?

Yes, but you must have repaid at least 50% of your current loan and have a good repayment history.
