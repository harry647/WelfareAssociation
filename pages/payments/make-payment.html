How to Make a Payment

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Select Payment Type - Choose your payment category (Contribution, Loan, Event, etc.) from the options above
  2. Fill in Your Details - Enter your Full Name, Student ID, Phone Number, and Amount
  3. Choose Payment Method - Select M-Pesa, Bank Transfer, Card Payment, or Cash
  4. Complete Payment - Follow the instructions for your chosen method below
  5. Get Confirmation - You'll receive an SMS confirmation and receipt

M-Pesa STK Push (Recommended - Fastest)

  1. Enter your phone number in the Phone Number field (format: 2547XXXXXXXX)
  2. Click the green "Pay via M-Pesa (STK Push)" button
  3. Wait for the M-Pesa prompt on your phone (you'll receive an SMS)
  4. Enter your M-Pesa PIN and press OK to confirm
  5. You'll receive a confirmation SMS with your transaction details
  6. Your payment will be automatically recorded - no need to enter Transaction ID

This method is instant and recommended for fastest payment processing.

M-Pesa Manual Payment

  1. Go to M-Pesa on your phone
  2. Select "Lipa na M-Pesa"
  3. Select "Pay Bill"
  4. Enter Business Number: 123456
  5. Enter Account Number: Your Member ID (e.g., JOOUST/2024/001)
  6. Enter the Amount and confirm
  7. You will receive an SMS confirmation with Transaction ID
  8. Enter the Transaction ID in the Transaction ID field above
  9. Click "Submit Payment" to complete

Card Payment (Visa/Mastercard)

  1. Select "Card Payment" as the payment method
  2. Click "Submit Payment" button
  3. You will be redirected to a secure payment gateway
  4. Enter your card details (card number, expiry, CVV)
  5. Complete the transaction with your bank's OTP verification
  6. You'll receive a confirmation and be redirected back

Cash Payment

  1. Select "Cash" as the payment method
  2. Click "Submit Payment"
  3. Visit the SWA office during working hours
  4. Pay the exact amount to the finance team
  5. Get a physical receipt for your records

Important Notes

  • All payments must include your Member ID as reference
  • Save your Transaction ID as proof of payment
  • You'll receive an SMS confirmation once payment is processed
  • For any issues, contact the finance team via the Contact page
  • Payments are processed within 24-48 hours
