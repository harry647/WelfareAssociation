As an SWA Member, You'll Get:

  • Access to interest-free loans

  • Academic mentorship programs
  • Career development workshops
  • Networking events

Eligibility

  • Must be a registered JOOUST student
  • Valid student ID required
  • Willing to make monthly contributions
  • Agree to abide by SWA policies
